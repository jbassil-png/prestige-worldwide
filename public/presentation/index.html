Prestige Worldwide

Cross-border financial planning for expats, dual citizens, and global citizens.

Live MVP Next.js · Supabase · OpenRouter
The Problem

Your money lives in
multiple countries.
Your plan doesn't.

Expats and dual citizens hold assets across jurisdictions — different currencies, tax regimes, and retirement systems. No single tool brings it together.

🌍

Multiple jurisdictions

401(k) in the US. RRSP in Canada. ISA in the UK. No unified view.

💱

Currency complexity

Savings in USD, GBP, CAD — no tool tracks net worth across them coherently.

📋

Fragmented advice

Each country's advisors only see their slice. Nobody sees the full picture.

The Solution

One plan.
Every country.

Prestige Worldwide connects your accounts across borders, generates a unified AI-powered retirement strategy, and gives you an advisor who speaks your full financial picture.

🤖

AI Plan Generation

Claude generates a personalised cross-border retirement plan from your goals and assets.

🏦

Account Aggregation

Connect bank accounts via Plaid or enter manually. Multi-currency, multi-country.

💬

Chat Advisor

Ask questions about your plan in plain language. Context-aware, always available.

AI Architecture

Three models. Three jobs.

Claude 3.5 Haiku
via OpenRouter · JSON mode
Plan generation + chat. Structured JSON output for the retirement plan. Streaming for the chat advisor. Fast and cost-efficient — runs on every dashboard refresh.
Gemini Flash 1.5
via OpenRouter · hardcoded
Portfolio insights. Lightweight daily market summaries. Speed-optimised, low-stakes — ideal for high-frequency, lower-complexity inference.
OpenRouter
Unified AI gateway
Model abstraction layer. All AI calls go through OpenRouter — model-agnostic, swappable via env var. No vendor lock-in. Started with N8N workflows, migrated to direct API calls.
