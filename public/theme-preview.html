How does the US-UK tax treaty affect my retirement withdrawals?
Great question. Under the US-UK treaty, your 401(k) withdrawals are generally only taxable in the US if you're a US citizen, even if you're resident in the UK at retirement. However, state-level taxes can still apply, and the UK may tax the growth if you've been UK-resident during accumulation. It's worth getting advice on your domicile status well before 2045.
